# Discussion: Diversity in Tech

## Reading

### 1. Consider the history

**Read:**  
**That Time When Women Stopped Coding**  
[The Time When Women Stopped Coding](https://www.npr.org/sections/money/2014/10/21/357629765/when-women-stopped-coding)

After reading, discuss the following questions:

1. What occurred during the same time as the beginning of the decline of women in computer science?
2. Why does it matter that males had been playing on computers growing up?

---

### 2. Review the data

**Explore:**  
**Employee Breakdown of Key Technology Companies**  
[Tech Companies](https://informationisbeautiful.net/visualizations/diversity-in-tech/)

Take a few minutes to examine the charts and trends. Be prepared to discuss what you notice about representation across different technology companies.

---

### 3. Ask the question

**Read:**  
**Why Diversity Matters to Your Tech Company**  
[Diversity Matters](https://www.usatoday.com/story/tech/columnist/2015/07/21/why-diversity-matters-your-tech-company/30419871/)

After reading, discuss the following questions:

1. When are diversity efforts most successful?
2. Why do diverse companies perform better?
3. Give an example of how a diverse company can better serve a diverse user base, or how a diverse user base benefits from having a diverse company.

---

## Discussion Guidelines

- Support your answers with examples from the readings.
- Listen respectfully to different perspectives.
- Be prepared to explain your reasoning.
- Consider how diversity can impact teamwork, creativity, and product design in the technology industry.
